Spread a stick of cream cheese in a 9 by 9 baking dish.
Combine refried beans, diced white onion and pickled jalapenos in a bowl. 
Heat and then spread on top of the cream cheese.  
Top with diced tomatos and more jalapenos.
Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
Heat in oven until warm.
Broil to melt cheese.
Serve with tortilla chips.
